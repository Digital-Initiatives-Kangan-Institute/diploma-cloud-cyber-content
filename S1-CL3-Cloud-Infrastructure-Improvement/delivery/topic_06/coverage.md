# Topic 6 — Writing the IaC by component: leading, supporting & monitoring the team · Coverage

**Topic 6 of 8** · **AT2 content Topic** (Team Implementation — run the build; 401 el 3–4, NOT 504-assessed) · teaching source: BSBXTW401 leadership content (coaching, feedback, conflict, development) + bespoke (leading a divided-IaC write) · *DRAFT 2026-07-02*.

This file is the **coverage spec** for the Topic.

**Depth ceiling: LEADERSHIP.** Lead, support and monitor the team *while* it writes the CloudFormation divided by component. The IaC write is the vehicle; the assessed evidence is the leadership (BSBXTW401) exercised through it — coaching, feedback, conflict handling, and development. Not 504-assessed; the technical deploy is AT3.

---

## What this Topic must cover

The delivery half of AT2: run the team through the write of the improved-architecture IaC — network / compute / database / storage, one component per member, integrated into one template. Coach and support individuals, facilitate issue resolution and problem-solving, measure performance against the work plans, give feedback, and identify and implement development opportunities. Four components:

- **C1 — Coaching & supporting.** Provide coaching to enhance workplace culture; support individuals per organisational requirements toward the common goals — using mentoring/coaching techniques and modelling professional leadership behaviours as the team writes its components.
- **C2 — Facilitating issue resolution & problem-solving.** Facilitate the team to identify, brainstorm, report and resolve task-related issues and inefficiencies; use problem-solving skills for team/task/individual challenges; manage conflicts and challenges per organisational requirements — using conflict-resolution/negotiation strategies and handling the teamwork challenges (task difficulties, conflicts, risks/hazards, unethical/inappropriate behaviour).
- **C3 — Measuring performance & feedback.** Measure member performance against the agreed work plans; provide timely, constructive feedback to organisational standards; provide feedback and assistance to members.
- **C4 — Development opportunities & action plans.** Identify learning and development opportunities to improve team/individual performance; implement action plans for training needs; collate feedback on individual and team performance and implement development opportunities for others.
