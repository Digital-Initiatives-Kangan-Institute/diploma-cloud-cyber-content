This file is the **coverage spec** for the Topic.

**Depth ceiling: LEADERSHIP / PLAN.** Plan the team and set it up to build — objectives, performance plans, accountability, contingencies, and the allocation of the four IaC components to members. This is leadership evidence (BSBXTW401), not technical (504) evidence; the build itself runs in Topic 6.

---

## What this Topic must cover

The set-up half of AT2: establish the team for the IaC build and allocate the work. Identify common objectives and responsibilities, set per-member performance expectations, choose accountability strategies, plan contingencies, communicate the objectives, and **allocate the four components — network / compute / database / storage — one per member** with appropriate instruction, facilitating respectful collaboration across diverse members. Four components:

- **C1 — Objectives, performance plans & accountability.** Identify common team objectives, responsibilities and outcomes; use performance plans to set per-member expected outcomes/goals/behaviours; select accountability strategies — grounded in the organisational requirements (policies, codes of conduct, culture) and the legislative requirements relevant to the workplace.
- **C2 — Contingency planning.** Plan for contingencies that could impact the team — the typical workplace contingencies (unplanned leave/absence, re-allocation of tasks, succession planning for key roles).
- **C3 — Communicating & allocating the component work.** Communicate the common objectives and responsibilities; allocate tasks (the four IaC components) to members by expertise or development potential, with appropriate instruction and any required contingencies.
- **C4 — Facilitating collaboration.** Facilitate open and respectful communication and collaboration across diverse backgrounds; identify cross-collaboration opportunities with internal and external teams — using facilitation techniques, varied communication methods/styles, and cross-cultural / special-needs communication principles.
