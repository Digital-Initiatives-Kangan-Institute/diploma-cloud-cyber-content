# S1-CL1 Cloud Design & Build — Cluster Specification
> **STATUS: AGREED 2026-05-31.** Semester S1 · Units: ICTCLD502, ICTCLD401, ICTICT517

The agreed delivery frame for the cluster — the box the teaching is fitted into. Reverse-engineered
from the delivered S1-CL1 plan to back-test the format and the linter; the numbers are the ones the
delivery was actually built against (see `delivery/planning/cl1-delivery-sessions-draft.md`).

## 1. Delivery frame
- Nominal hours: 104
- Weeks: 8
- Sessions per week: 4
- Session length (hours): 3
- Total sessions: 32
- Delivered hours: 96
- Variance (nominal − delivered): +8
- Over-nominal authorisation: n/a
- Sequencing: weeks 1–8; delivered solo (the only cluster running, before CL2 + CL3 begin in week 9)

## 2. Schedule conventions
- Onboarding session: yes — S1 (orientation, set students up on the systems, email + teaching-material access, scenario introduction; no teaching)
- Spare/catch-up buffer: yes — the final day (S31 + S32): resits of failed assessments + overflow time; students who have finished may skip the final day
- Final-assessment placement: AT3 completes at S30 (the day immediately before the spare buffer); AT1 + AT2 sit within the S2–S28 block
- Session rhythm: AM/PM pairs on the same day (session 1 = AM, session 2 = PM); 2 teaching days/week across weeks 1–8

## 3. Topic budget
- Onboarding sessions: 1
- Spare sessions: 2
- Dedicated assessment sessions: 2
- Teaching/practice sessions available: 27
- Nominal topic count: 14

*The 27 available sessions are the S2–S28 block; AT1 + AT2 assessment time is interleaved within it
(only AT3's prep + practical, S29–S30, are dedicated assessment sessions). 14 content Topics were
derived — AT1 → Topics 1–5, AT2 → Topics 6–10, AT3 → Topics 11–14.*

## 4. Open questions / TBDs
- None. The frame is settled and the cluster's delivery materials are built against it.
